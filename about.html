Welcome to MAGYS
This application consist of two main menus:
  • Find Rent Room
  • Choose Query Rent Room

Find Rent Room can help you to find information and location of rent room in South Jakarta. You can find a rent room based on 3 parameters:

  • Price.
  • Distance from Universitas Bakrie.
  • Facilities such as Wi-Fi, Kitchen, and AC.

Choose Query Rent Room can help you to get additional information that shows analysis result from all rent rooms information in South Jakarta.
