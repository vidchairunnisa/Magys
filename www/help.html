Help
To use this application...
You can choose "Find Rent Room tab" or "Choose Query Rent Room tab" in the bottom of this screen.

  • If you choose "Find Rent Room tab" :

    • Click filter button.
    • Choose parameter by click the parameters.
    • Click search button.
    • And voilla! Your room rent will be appear in map if it's available.

  • If you choose "Choose Query Rent Room tab" :

    • Choose and Click a query.
    • And voilla! The result of map based on query that you choose will be appear.

  • Caution :
  • If application can't operate or getting stuck suddenly, you can exit from this apllication by back to your home of your device
